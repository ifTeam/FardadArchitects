FARDAD GHASEMZADEH

Founder

Fardad Architects, founded in 2018 by Fardad Ghasemzadeh, specializes in innovative, functional, and sustainable architectural solutions. With expertise in Digital Architecture and cutting-edge design, the studio focuses on creating aesthetically compelling and highly functional spaces. Their approach emphasizes shaping environments that enhance living and working experiences through attention to detail, client collaboration, and a balance of creativity, functionality, and sustainability.
